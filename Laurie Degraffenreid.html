LAURIE DEGRAFFENREID

PARALEGAL

Ms. DeGraffenreid is a landman and paralegal with extensive experience in energy. She has over 20 years of expertise with East Texas, where she researched title, negotiated leases, supervised crews, and managed the Tyler office of Ergon Exploration Company. She assists in all aspects of land and title examination, including curative, runsheets and preparation of divisions of interest.
